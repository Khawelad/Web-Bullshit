About Us

Nextag was officially launched in 2021 as a retailer of premium quality leather goods, founded by Mr. Anas. Nextag is the flagship brand of [Example], an organization with a long heritage in leather & textile manufacturing, tracing its foundation back to a leather trading house set up in the early 1900s.

At Nextag, a discerning clientele can choose from a wide variety of leather product lines including but not limited to sophisticated wallets, premium office bags and women's purses. We put a high premium on design and attention to detail with the aid of a skilled & motivated team of experts and technological support. This professionally managed setup is sensitive to the needs of its national & international customers. Nextag has a growing retail presence in 4 major cities and online in order to provide service across Pakistan and beyond.
